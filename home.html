Zedargo Bendugar

Former Jedi Padawan, now a criminal gambler

Strength: 15
Dexterity: 13
Constitution: 14
Intelligence: 16
Wisdom: 8
Charisma: 18
  • Place of Birth: Malastare
  • Personality Traits: A free-spirited adrenaline junkie with a gambling addiction and a bit of a temper problem
  • Ideals: Where's the thrill in life without risk?
  • Bonds: Zed's master, Jin'daar Puulkin, was taken by bounty hunters and he left the Jedi Order to find out where and why he was taken.
  • Flaws: Zed can get irrationally angry when talked down to. He is also dependent on deathsticks to control his temper.

Backstory:

Zed joined the Jedi as a toddler after his parents gave him over to Jedi Seekers that visited his homeworld of Malastare. He struggled with the training which created insecurities that would play into his temperment issues. Zed's master, a Cerean named Jin'Daar Puulkin, was taken by bounty hunters while they were on a mercy mission to Malastare. In a blinding hate-fuled rampage, Zed killed all of them except for their leader, who was able to make their escape with Jin'Daar in tow.

Zed left the Jedi Order to search for his master after the Jedi Council forbade him from doing so. He spent the next year searching for leads on his masters whereabouts by mingling in the casinos in Coruscant's underbelly. In a stroke of luck one night, Zed encountered a Trandoshan slaver named Aska Drenqisch and challenged her to a game of pazzak. As part of her wager, Aska revealed that she was the one who kidnapped Zed's master. Zed knew he would have to fight Aska and her crew to get to his master, but he couldn't do it alone.

Knowing he was outmatched, Zed sought an alliance with discraced Karkarodon mob boss, Wilson Fishk. Zed offered his force powers and abilities in exchange for protection as part of his gang. The plan was simple, lead Wilson's party into conflict with Aska and take Jin'Daar in the shroud of chaos.
